About MediPredict AI

Discover the technology, research-backing, and algorithms driving our risk prediction models.

Our Vision

To build a highly accessible, rapid, and precise preliminary health screening layer that flags potential chronic health conditions, enabling patients to seek professional diagnostics and intervention early.

Our Mission

To demystify clinical indicators. By giving patients an understanding of how parameters like blood glucose, cholesterol, and serum creatinine relate to disease progression, we bridge the gap between complex biochemistry and personal health awareness.

Under the Hood: Machine Learning Pipeline

MediPredict AI integrates two prominent machine learning methodologies to compute its assessments:

Logistic Regression

A parametric classification algorithm that models the probability of a binary outcome using a logistic sigmoid function. Highly interpretable, it establishes clear feature importance weights.

Random Forest Classifier

An ensemble learning method consisting of numerous decision trees. By averaging predictions across trees, it handles non-linear relationships and interactions with high robustness, avoiding overfitting.

Typical Data Processing Pipeline
  1. Data Standardisation: Raw user inputs are scaled using `StandardScaler` to have zero mean and unit variance, matching the distribution of model training cohorts.
  2. Model Training: Classifiers are optimized using 80/20 train/test splits, recording precision, recall, and ROC AUC metrics.
  3. Probability Calibration: `predict_proba()` produces the exact risk score, mapping features to a percentage risk.
  4. Dual Verification: The alternative model is evaluated simultaneously to highlight any differences in classification confidence.
